Torment #1: The Story Begins

Spiderman #1 begins with a classic introduction to the hero, as Spiderman saves another person from danger. Peter Parker returns to comfort his wife, Mary Jane, as she often worries about him and the risks he takes as Spiderman.
Meanwhile, darknees looms as a familiar foe of Spiderman, the Lizard, lurks in the shadows of the night in New York City. The Lizard hunts down and kills 3 victims by clashing them to death, which gets the attention of our Friendly, Neighborhood Spiderman.
